Treatment Methodologies

We educate patients on how to take care of themselves and perform exercises on their own so they are able to move and function with ease in their daily lives.

Neuromuscular

The mind and body have a strong connection and our facility focuses on therapy techniques to return our patients back to functioning using step by step processes to make the mind-body connection stronger. Not only do our programs improve your physical status, but also your focus and coordination.

Therapeutic Exercises

Every patient is given a personalized therapeutic exercise plan to fit their individual needs. Our therapist gives you the best exercises to utilize your time while you are in the clinic and also gives you the best exercises to do at home in order to maximize your treatment and benefit.

Manual Therapy

Every patient receives hands on care so that our therapist can feel what is going on, and be able to pin point the certain areas/joints that need physical therapy. Our certified therapist uses different manual therapy techniques such as soft tissue, deep tissue, cross friction, mobilization and stabilization.
